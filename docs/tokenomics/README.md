# $UPLIFT Token Economics

## Token Function

$UPLIFT serves as the economic layer for the platform, used for:
- Compute events
- Purchases
- Premium features
- Governance

## Important Clarification

$UPLIFT does **not** represent:
- Equity in Operator Uplift
- Ownership in the company
- Governance or voting rights
- Claims on company revenue

## Investment Structure

The company is conducting a pre-seed equity round, and investors will be buying equity, not tokens.

## Long-term Vision

Future plans for $UPLIFT involve:
- Continued use as internal computation/credits
- Potential future exploration of more formal connections to usage 
- Ecosystem incentives

The company maintains a clear separation between equity and $UPLIFT to ensure transparency for investors and regulators.
