# Operator Uplift

## Company Overview
Operator Uplift is developing an agent-native operating system for AI agents, aiming to unify them in a trusted platform with shared private memory. 

## Leadership
- Matt Sim (Co-founder & CEO)
- Dhruv Aggarwal (Co-founder & CTO)
- Matus Remis (Co-founder & COO)

## Launch Timeline
- Private Pre-MVP: November 2025
- Public MVP: December 10, 2025
  - Initial agents include: FileUplifter, AgenticWallet, Gmail Agent, and Google Calendar Agent

## Strategic Collaborations
Operator Uplift is engaged in strategic collaborations, including with Meteora, and leveraging hackathons to expand its agent ecosystem.

## Funding
The company is raising a $500K USD pre-seed round on a $5M valuation cap.
